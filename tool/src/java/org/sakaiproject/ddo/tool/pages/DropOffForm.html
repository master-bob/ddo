Expectations of DDO Service

Write Place consultants aim to help you become a stronger writer; therefore, we are not an editing service. Rather, a Write Place consultant will review your paper as an active reader and help you understand if your writing is clear and engaging. Consultants will help identify patterns of errors and make suggestions on how to correct them. A Write Place consultant will spend 30 minutes on 5 pages of your document. Comments and suggested corrections will be provided by using the revision tool in MS Word. A response to your paper can be expected within 48 hours. Any information you can offer regarding the writing assignment or your areas of concern will be beneficial to the consultant.

Please submit each paper only once. You may upload one writing assignment at a time.

Questions? Email the Write Place at writeplace@udayton.edu or contact the Write Place Coordinator at cklimo1@udayton.edu.
